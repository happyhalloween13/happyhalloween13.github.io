SMASHBURGERS

Smashburgers - A great summertime favorite for the family, consisting of juicy and tasty goodness!

"Smash Burgers are not a thick, exquisitely grilled, medium-rare burger. They are not a lovingly crafted, perfectly symmetrical patty made with the most expensive cuts of beef and a heavily guarded spice mix. They are minced beef and salt, smashed flat over a searingly hot flat-grill until you get a crispy, craggy-edged, asymmetric, cooked-through patty that is juicy, caramelised, beefy and, frankly, the best burger you are ever going eat."

    Ingredients
  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Condiments / Toppings
      • Steps
    1. Roll ground beef into medium sized balls
    2. Place beef balls in pan with oil on high heat
    3. Smash beef balls with flat surface i.e. spatula, until flattened thin
    4. Cook for approx. 1 minute on each side
    5. Garnish with your choice of toppings / condiments. Enjoy!
