Microwave Nachos

Microwave nachos are simple, fool-proof and delicious!

For easy snacks, nothing beats a plate of microwaved nachos! These are no frills, easy to make, and delicious. Plus, these are ready in literally 2 minutes.

    Ingredients
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese of choice
  • Toppings of choice
      • Steps
    1. Place tortilla chips and cheese of choice on microwave safe plate
    2. Microwave plate for 2 minutes or until preferred
    3. Add toppings of choice. Enjoy!
