Grilled Cheese

Grilled Cheese - A simple American classsic that everyone loves.

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich that has been around since 1920. It is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread and originally filled with American cheese, but is now commonly made with one or more different cheeses.

    Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
      • Steps
    1. Butter your bread evenly
    2. Place preferred cheese of choice between bread
    3. Place bread on hot pan and evenly grill until preferred gooeyness. Enjoy!
